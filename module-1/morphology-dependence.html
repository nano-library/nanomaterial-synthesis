✨ Morphology dependence refers to the phenomenon where physical, chemical, optical, and magnetic properties of materials change dramatically as their shape and structure are varied at the nanoscale.

Shape-Dependent Optical Properties

🟩
Influence of shape
Beyond spherical quantum dots, the shape of a nanostructure also tunes its optical response through the localised surface plasmon resonance (LSPR) effect.
Nanorods

Aspect ratio controls the colour. Increasing the aspect ratio red-shifts the longitudinal plasmon band, from blue to red across the visible spectrum.

Nanoshells

Shell thickness relative to core size tunes the optical response. Thinner shells shift absorption to longer wavelengths, from blue-violet through to near-infrared.
